# Richtlinien für Beratungsaufträge

**Issuer**: Wirtschaftskammer Tirol
**Version**: 01.10.2023
**Purpose**: Rules for consultants executing WK-commissioned consulting

---

## 1. Allgemeines (General)

These guidelines apply to consulting commissioned by WK Tirol on behalf of the companies being advised.

**Consequences of non-compliance**: Exclusion from the Beraterpool (consultant pool).

**Important**:
- No legal claim to receive consulting assignments from accepting these guidelines
- Not permitted to call yourself "WK-Berater" or "Konsulent der WK"

### 1.1 Einsatz von Berater:innen

- Use of employees/partners allowed if:
  - They are listed in the Beraterpool
  - WK is notified
  - WK gives consent
- WK may request or reject specific consultants
- Commissioned consultant remains responsible and liable

**Prohibitions**:
- Direct contracts between company and consultant CANNOT be funded retroactively
- Starting consulting without WK assignment = consultant bears full risk of payment collection

### 1.2 Interessenkonflikt, Werbung, Unvereinbarkeit

**Before consulting**:
- Check for existing contracts between client and other consultants
- Notify WK of potential conflicts (e.g., competing clients)

**During consulting - PROHIBITED**:
- Direct or indirect advertising, sales, or brokerage activities
- Specific product promotion (hardware, software, machines, services)
- Accepting assignments where close ties to providers prevent neutral advice

**Recommendations allowed**:
- Further consulting and training can be recommended
- Must be documented in report as separate item
- Non-funded follow-up contracts must be clearly separated from funded consulting
- No temporal overlap between funded and unfunded consulting at same company

**Scope limitation**:
- Only address questions within consultant's expertise and authorization
- Cannot perform work legally reserved for other professions

### 1.3 Verschwiegenheit (Confidentiality)

Absolute confidentiality required regarding:
- The consulting assignment
- All facts and data about the consulting and company

**Exception**: Reporting obligation to WK Tirol

Data protection regulations must be observed.

### 1.4 Kontakt mit Mitarbeiter:innen

Contact with client's employees or external parties (banks, accountants, suppliers) requires approval from the authorized representative of the company being advised.

## 2. Beratungsablauf (Consulting Process)

### 2.1 Auftragsvergabe/Auftragsannahme

WK assignment is issued in writing, in the name and on account of the company being advised.

### 2.2 Beratungsvorgespräch (Initial Meeting)

At the start of consulting, consultant must establish with the client:
- Consulting goal
- Measures to verify goal achievement
- Expected time expenditure (max. 20% deviation allowed)
- Appropriate consulting process
- **Binding offer** for expected consulting costs

The maximum funding amount is communicated to the client in the WK assignment letter.

### 2.3 Durchführung der Beratung (Execution)

**Starting**:
- Only after written agreement on goals, process, time, and costs

**During consulting**:
- If >20% deviation from original agreement: immediately consult with client and WK
- If reasons arise for early termination: contact client and WK immediately
- Consultant may terminate if client doesn't provide necessary documents/information

**Timeline**:
- Must be completed within **8 months** of assignment
- If not achievable: contact WK immediately
- For consulting >5 months: client and WK may request interim reports

**Location**:
- Preferably at client's premises (if practical and cost-effective)
- Should be holistically aligned with client's circumstances and capabilities

**Collaboration**:
- If needed, contact client's accountant or other advisors

### 2.4 Schlussbesprechung (Final Meeting)

After consulting completion:
- Present results in final meeting
- Explain recommended measures in detail
- Document in written consulting report (Beratungsbericht)
- Reference other WK/WIFI support offerings if useful

**Cancellation**:
If consulting not started within 6 weeks OR not completed within 8 months due to consultant's reasons:
- WK may cancel assignment (after discussion with consultant and client agreement)
- Consultant cannot charge any costs to client or WK

## 3. Bericht/Beratungsabschluss (Report/Completion)

### 3.1 Beratungsbericht Structure

According to WK quality criteria, in language understandable to the client:

1. **General data**: Consulting company
2. **General data**: Company being advised
3. **Inhaltsverzeichnis** (Table of contents)
4. **Beratungsauftrag und Beratungsziel** (Assignment and goals)
5. **Beratungsablauf** (Process description - key points)
6. **Ausführliche Problembeschreibung** / IST-Zustand (Befund)
7. **Beratungsergebnis** (Results)
8. **Maßnahmen und Realisierungsempfehlungen** (who, what, when + cost estimates)
9. **Entscheidungsgrundlagen und Lösungswege** - Tables, charts, flip-chart copies, descriptions
   - **This is the CORE of the consulting**
10. **For Jungunternehmen**: Plankostenrechnung incl. Mindestumsatzrechnung, Deckungsbeitragsrechnung, Break-Even-Analyse
11. **For investment decisions**: Multiple variants (pessimistic/optimistic/likely) to show risk profile
12. **Freizeichnungsklausel** (Disclaimer): "Implementation of recommended measures must be done by the company itself and in its own responsibility. All suggestions were compiled by the consultant in good faith, based on operational review, with provided information and documents."
13. **Consultant's signature** (handwritten)
14. **Appendix**: All documents created during consulting

**Scope**: Must align with WK-provided checklists for each consulting focus area.

### Financial Difficulties Warning

If consulting reveals serious financial difficulties suggesting insolvency risk:
- Immediately consult with WK
- Explicitly note this separately in the consulting report

### Document Handling

- All received documents and presentations must be provided to WK on request with the report
- All created documents (not part of report) must be returned to client after completion
- Results available to client exclusively for internal purposes (unlimited use rights)
- Client may use results in official proceedings
- Usage rights paid with consulting fee

### 3.2 Recht auf Einwendungen (Right to Object)

- WK may object to consulting results and report structure
- If WK objects: consultant may not pursue fee collection from client until issues resolved
- Objection has court-suspending effect
- Consultant must respond and seek agreement with client and WK
- Fachverband Unternehmensberatung available for difficult cases
- WK may request free rework if required by quality criteria

### 3.3 Abrechnung/Auftragsabschluss (Billing/Completion)

**Fee rates**: WK rates valid at assignment apply for entire consulting, regardless of completion date.

**After final meeting**, consultant sends to WK:
- Consulting report with all attachments
- Invoice (Honorarnote)
- Time record (Stundenaufzeichnung)
- Final meeting confirmation (Bestätigung der Endbesprechung)

**Invoice requirements**:
- Issued to client's name
- Include assignment number
- Structured per template (Musterrechnung)
- Detailed service information in attachment (dates, routes, etc.)

**Partial invoices**: Allowed in justified cases (long consulting duration) with interim report.

**Process**:
1. WK checks report against goals and quality criteria
2. If positive: forwards to client
3. Client accepts report → assignment completed, invoice due
4. Client pays full invoice to consultant
5. Client sends payment proof (Einzahlungsbeleg or ELBA printout) to WK
6. WK pays funding to client

**WK liability**: None for client payment. If client doesn't pay despite collection efforts, consultant must notify WK before legal action.

**After completion**: Consultant free to make further agreements with client independent of WK guidelines.

### 3.4 Honorarausfälle (Fee Losses)

WK Tirol assumes **no liability** for consultant fees lost due to insolvency proceedings (Sanierungsverfahren, Konkursverfahren, dismissal for lack of assets) of the company being advised.
